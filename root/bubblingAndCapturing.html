Bubble and Capture

what you want to see click below first before click on Root or children Nodes

To reverse the natural order of event propagation (bubbling followed by capturing), you can't change the internal event propagation mechanism because the DOM always executes the capturing phase first, followed by the bubbling phase. However, you can simulate this reversed behavior by delaying the execution of the capturing phase using setTimeout.
